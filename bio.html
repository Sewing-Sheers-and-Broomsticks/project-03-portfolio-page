About the Artist

Brynna Perry is a Maryland based, 2nd year Graphic Design student with ambitions to become a theme park designer for Universal Creative. Through she is currently focusing on graphic design Brynna enjoys trying new art mediums whenever she can. Her areas of interest to include, digital art, fashion design, fiber arts, jewelry making, book binding, stamp making, and oil painting. Her main inspiration for her artwork often stems from a desire for a particular item and not being able to find it or wanting to find creative ways use left over supplies from other projects, her work in heavily inspired by the fantasy genre.
